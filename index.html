Welcome to The Belly Button's Bacterial Database
The colony of bacterial species from humane belly button that could make up an ideal synthesized beef!

Synthesizing a tasty beef with the bacterial species from the source as close as the one on our humane body? We might make this possible!

In partner with Improbable Beef, a rising food Startup, our laboratory has been collecting the bacterial species from various belly buttons of people across the country!

This dashboard provides the visualization of the bacterial species found in each volunteer (refer by the ID), as well as their demographic info.
By selecting the Test Subject ID, we can access the visualizations of their belly button's bacterial colonies!
